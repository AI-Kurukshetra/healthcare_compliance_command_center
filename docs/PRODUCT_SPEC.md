# Product Specification

Product Name:
Healthcare Compliance Management Platform

Purpose:
Help healthcare SMBs manage regulatory compliance automatically.

Target Users:
- Clinics
- Medical labs
- Dental practices
- Telehealth companies

Core Problems Solved:

- Complex HIPAA compliance requirements
- Lack of compliance teams in SMB healthcare businesses
- Increasing cybersecurity threats
- Expensive regulatory penalties

Core Features:

1 HIPAA Compliance Assessment
2 Risk Management Dashboard
3 Policy Template Library
4 Employee Training Management
5 Incident Response Workflow
6 Audit Trail Generation
7 Vulnerability Scanning
8 Business Associate Agreement Management
9 Compliance Calendar
10 Documentation Repository
11 Risk Assessment Questionnaires
12 Regulatory Update Notifications
13 Access Control Management
14 Compliance Reporting Suite
15 Data Breach Response Plan
16 Vendor Risk Assessment
17 Mobile Security Management
18 Email Encryption Monitoring

User Roles:

Admin
Compliance Officer
Employee
Auditor
